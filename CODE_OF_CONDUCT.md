# Community code of conduct

We are committed to a welcoming, respectful project.

Be kind, assume good intent, critique ideas rather than people, respect privacy,
and make space for people with different backgrounds and experience levels.
Harassment, hate speech, threats, sexualized attention, doxxing, and sustained
disruption are not accepted.

Maintainers may edit or remove contributions and restrict participation when
needed to protect the community. Report conduct concerns privately through the
contact method in [SECURITY.md](./SECURITY.md). Maintainers will protect the
reporter's privacy as far as practical.

This compact policy is inspired by the Contributor Covenant.
